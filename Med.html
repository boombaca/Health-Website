Meditation & Mental Health

Mental health is the main foundation for the well-being and effective functioning of individuals.Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood to adulthood. Mental health is a state of balance, both within and with the environment.More than 100 million people suffer from mental health disorders in the world. Annual spending on mental health is less than 20 rupees per person.

Research has found that meditation can have many health benefits, such as:


• Better focus and concentration
• Improve self-esteem and self-awareness
• Reduce stress
• Help manage anxiety or depression
• Keep calm and think using your brain in

Ways to meditate

-

1. Sit in a quiet space. Make sure there is nothing to disturb you.
2. Sit comfortably. Use a cushion, blanket, or chair. Sit straight and stay calm , your body should feel relaxed. Let loose of your body
3. Breathe gently. Focus your attention on your breathing. Alternatively, you can begin with focusing on each part of the body, down from your toes and up to your head, calming every body part of your body and letting it lose.
4. Let distractions come and go. Don’t think about anything and focus on your breath as you breath in and breath out
Do this for at least 10-15 min every day in the morning
